Study Debriefing

This study investigated how the communication style of an AI financial assistant — either formal and factual, or warm and conversational — affects participants' trust in the AI, their willingness to delegate decisions to it, and their self-regulation when evaluating AI recommendations. The two conditions you may have been assigned to were "low anthropomorphism" (professional and data-focused) and "high anthropomorphism" (friendly and empathetic). The AI assistant was powered by GPT-4o-mini, a large language model by OpenAI. All financial scenarios were fictitious. If you have any questions about this research, please contact the study team.
