AI Financial Assistant Study

Welcome! This study investigates how people interact with AI-powered financial assistants when making financial decisions. You will work through two realistic financial scenarios and answer some survey questions.

The study takes approximately 15 minutes to complete. Your responses are fully anonymous and participation is entirely voluntary.

What participation involves:

  • A brief pre-study questionnaire about your background.
  • Two interactive financial scenarios where you can consult an AI assistant.
  • A post-study questionnaire about your experience.
  • All responses are stored securely and anonymously.

Informed Consent

Please read the information above carefully before deciding whether to participate.
